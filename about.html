All About Bob

Captain Bob is a 65 year old native of South Florida. He has been fishing these waters since he was old enough to be on a boat. He spent most of his young adult life all around the world guiding and tournament fishing to include places like Costa Rica, Chili, Australia, and the Philippines. Captian Bob is married and has 2 daughters both in their 30s. Captain Bob decided to put his knowledge and skills he has gained from all of his experiences over the years, together to run his own charter fishing business. With you, the clients needs first, whether you like bottom fishing, trolling, fly fishing or bait casting. Bob is equiped to guarantee that you have a great tour and with 110% effort every time. Still young at heart and mind, Captain Bob has plenty of corky jokes to keep you smiling out on the water. Come have a taste of the wonderful experiences Bob has been sharing for the past 30 years.
